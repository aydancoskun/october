For Searchers:

If you are just searching by using OKTicK then there are advantages and good reasons for you to Register. It enables you to do much more in your searching:

1. You can save your specific Searches.
2. You can save Suppliers you may want to come back to.
3. Find specific Suppliers you are interested in.
4. Find Suppliers local to where you are.
5. Suggest Items that might be added to the system.

You can register by clicking here.

For Suppliers:

Here are some of the reasons to register:

1. You can input (or correct) your full contact details.
2. Correct your business name, if needed and provide details of your main line of activities.
3. It is important for your postcode to be on the system so we can determine if you are local to someone searching for a local supplier.
4. Add, delete or correct the list of products and services you provide.
5. Improve your placement in the list when your products or services are listed out.
6. Place images of your products and services onto the system.
7. Add videos of your products and services if you have them.
8. Place adverts for your products and services onto the website.

And many other things.

You can register by clicking here.
