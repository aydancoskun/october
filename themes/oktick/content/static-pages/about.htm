Taking more than 10 years to research and develop, a small British team have put together a unique approach to search for industrial, domestic and personal products and services.

This approach to finding what you want and locating suppliers is very fast and very direct. It is in fact what might be called the first Direct Search Engine.

The basis of OKTicK is that all products and services stem from a Keyword. A Keyword is the basic name for the item being searched for. For example if the item is a type of Table then the Keyword is “Tables" and items that stem from that are things like “Oak Tables", “Breakfast Tables" etc.

The items you will find are, basically, what might be called “Generic". That means they are 'general' not 'proprietary' (there is provision to proprietary names within the system and this will develop).

OKTicK employs sophisticated methods to locate Suppliers for the products and services you find here.

Any business or individual supplier can be on the system (Terms & Conditions apply) without charge.

Fees

In some cases to improve or ensure placement a fee is charged. "How much" is totally driven by “market forces". There are no artificially set levels. What suppliers decide to pay is the only regulating factor. (Of course to simplify matters the amount has to be in multiples of the lowest permitted stake - currently £1/week).

What you won't find on OKTicK

We do our very best to not host any websites who's goal is against the usual morals and norms. See Terms and Conditions for further information

Additional Features

OKTicK has many other features you might find useful.

You can find lists of Keywords of products and services that are Related to the item you searched for. For example if you searched for "Compressors" then related items might be "Pumps", "Blowers", "Fans", and "Turbines".

We also give definitions of Keywords (they are not "academic" but may help - see Terms & Conditions for limitations on these) Examples of items stemming from the Keyword have been included.

Also we have gathered some “Types" of things where this applies. For example if the Keyword is 'Cars' you will find 4x4s, Hatchbacks, Limousines, Saloons and SUVs etc under types.

There are other helpful features you will find too.

What OKTicK records

OKTicK does not record any of your searches or favourites in order to market any products and services or return advertisements of any sort (known loosely as 'cookies'. If you register you will be able to do so yourself if you wish.

Inappropriate Materials

We also won't permit on the system any sites that serve up pornographic, illegal or otherwise inappropriate materials. See Terms and Conditions for more details.
