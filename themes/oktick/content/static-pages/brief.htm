In OKTicK there are 2 key ways used to help you search for what you want. Each way has its own advantage. 

1. By Keyword

Put in your basic word, called the Keyword on OKTicK - Table, Chair, Equipment, Grinder etc. As you type OKTicK will try to predict your Keyword.

2. By Item

Just spell out what you are looking for in the usual way. The system will seek to predict what you are looking for.

As you progress with your search follow the guidance notes that appear in order to increase your chances of success.

For more detail see Search Tips

Generic vs Proprietary

OKTicK concentrates on the “general" or “generic" names for items, not particular manufacturer's name.
