OKTicK is a cross between a Search Engine and a Products & Services Directory.

It is designed to enable the rapid sourcing of commercial products and services. With its brand new approach and unique search design it enables Suppliers to be found very rapidly.

It is unique amongst search engines and we hope you find it valuable. It represents many years research by a small British team who took a fresh look and a new approach to the suppliers and searchers problem - easy finding and connection - uncluttered.
