From this page you can see what products or services we have in our records for your business.

Because these were semi-automatically collected initially there may be occassional errors. If any are incorrect you can delete them using the delete button and confirming deletion is your aim.

If find products or services are missing from your list, you can add them in first row below. Just enter your item, as you would normally in a search on OKTicK, select your choice from the list and click the ok button.

Please note that any product or service you propose to add will not show up immediately because we have to schedule verification. Naturally is our intent to ensure that only valid products and services are added to the OKTicK system.
