About OKTicK

OKTicK is a cross between a Search Engine and a Products & Services Directory. It is designed to enable the rapid sourcing of commercial products and services. This brand new approach enables Suppliers to be found very rapidly. It is unique amongst search engines. We hope you find it valuable. It represents many years research by a small British team who took a fresh look and a new approach to the suppliers and searchers problem - easy finding and connection.
